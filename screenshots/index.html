What this gives you

Running a music school usually means juggling a paper calendar, a spreadsheet of who paid what, a group chat for cancellations, and a notebook for "remind the Smiths their package is almost up." Crescendo replaces all of that.

📅

One calendar, zero double-booking

Every lesson, every teacher, every room — in one shared view.

💳

Always know who's paid

A clear answer to "has this student paid?" at any moment, with automatic reminders before a package runs out.

🙋

Self-service for families

Parents and students see their own schedule and balance, buy their next package, and request changes — without calling the office.

📝

Requests, not phone tag

New lessons, reschedules, and makeups come in as requests you approve or decline from one place.

⏱️

Cancellation policy, automatic

Notice windows, credits, and makeups enforced consistently — not remembered case by case.

💰

Payroll, calculated for you

Teacher pay is calculated automatically from the lessons actually taught.

How it works

The basics, the way an admin would actually use it day to day.

1

Add a student

Contact info, emergency contact, notes — with an optional portal invite for the student or a parent.

2

Sell a package

A package becomes a "term" — an active window with its own start date, end date, and locked-in rate.

3

Schedule lessons

Add a student, teacher, room, date, and time. Lessons can repeat weekly, and group sessions work the same way.

4

Attendance bills automatically

Marking a lesson present charges the student's active package at their locked-in rate — no manual invoicing.

5

Cancellations, handled

A configurable notice window decides what happens automatically — credits, makeups, or a charge.

6

Renewals, nudged

As a package runs low, a reminder email goes out — and stays on the dashboard until the student actually renews.
