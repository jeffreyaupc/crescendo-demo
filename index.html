What this gives you

Running a music school usually means juggling a paper calendar, a spreadsheet of who paid what, a group chat for cancellations, and a notebook for "remind the Smiths their package is almost up." Crescendo replaces all of that.

📅

One calendar, zero double-booking

Every lesson, every teacher, every room — in one shared view.

💳

Always know who's paid

A clear answer to "has this student paid?" at any moment, with automatic reminders before a package runs out.

🙋

Self-service for families

Parents and students see their own schedule and balance, buy their next package, and request changes — without calling the office.

📝

Requests, not phone tag

New lessons, reschedules, and makeups come in as requests you approve or decline from one place.

⏱️

Cancellation policy, automatic

Notice windows, credits, and makeups enforced consistently — not remembered case by case.

💰

Payroll, calculated for you

Teacher pay is calculated automatically from the lessons actually taught.

A look inside

A few of the screens your front desk, teachers, and families would actually use day to day.

Admin Dashboard

An at-a-glance view of today's lessons, pending requests, and lessons that have drifted past a student's package end date — the day's to-do list, already built.

Schedule

One calendar for every lesson, every teacher, every room — color-coded by teacher so conflicts and gaps are obvious at a glance.

Student Profile

Each student's packages, balance, and lesson history in one place — no digging through spreadsheets to answer "where do they stand?"

Requests Queue

Reschedules, makeups, and new-lesson requests from families and teachers land in one queue. Accept & schedule in one click — the lesson form comes prefilled from the request. Free lessons the school owes are tracked right below until they're honored.

Payments

Every dollar in, in one ledger — who paid, how (cash, card, e-transfer, Stripe), and for what. Filter by month or source, and totals update live.

Instrument Rentals

Rent keyboards, violins, and amps to students or walk-ins. Deposits held, expected returns, and actual revenue per rental — tracked without touching tuition balances.

Parent Portal

Parents see the balance, the next lesson, and one-tap actions — request a lesson, a makeup, or a reschedule. Families with multiple children switch between them in one account.

Balance & Packages

Families see exactly where their package stands — lessons used, amount remaining, and when to renew — and can buy the next package online. No phone call required.

Teacher Portal

Teachers get their own week at a glance, take attendance, leave lesson notes, and see their pay add up — without needing anything from the front desk.

How it works

The basics, the way an admin would actually use it day to day.

1

Add a student

Contact info, emergency contact, notes — with an optional portal invite for the student or a parent.

2

Sell a package

A package becomes a "term" — an active window with its own start date, end date, and locked-in rate.

3

Schedule lessons

Add a student, teacher, room, date, and time. Lessons can repeat weekly, and group sessions work the same way.

4

Attendance bills automatically

Marking a lesson present charges the student's active package at their locked-in rate — no manual invoicing.

5

Cancellations, handled

A configurable notice window decides what happens automatically — credits, makeups, or a charge.

6

Renewals, nudged

As a package runs low, a reminder email goes out — and stays on the dashboard until the student actually renews.
